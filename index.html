Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
If you signed up with email, use the "Forgot Password" link on the sign-in screen. If you signed in with Apple or Google, manage your password through that provider directly.

How do I delete my account or data?
Email us at the address above with your account email and a request to delete your account. We'll confirm once your data has been removed.

How do I update my profile photo or logs?
You can update your profile photo and edit step or goal logs directly within the app from your profile and log screens.

I found a bug or have a feature request.
We'd love to hear about it — email us a description of what happened (and a screenshot if you have one) and we'll look into it.

Contact Us

For anything not covered above, email support.burn@gmail.com and we'll help however we can.
